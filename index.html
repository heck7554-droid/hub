Where to find these links:
• Google Calendar (on a computer): Settings → your calendar → Integrate calendar → Secret address in iCal format.
• iPhone/iCloud: Calendar app → Calendars → tap ⓘ next to a calendar → turn on Public Calendar → Share Link.
• School / sports sites: look for a “Subscribe”, “iCal”, or “Add to calendar” link.
